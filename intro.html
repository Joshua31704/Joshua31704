Bad.. Menswear..?

What makes.. bad menswear? How does once create such a failed aesthetic? While it's fair that one can say, "oh, such silly things are subjective," there are some things that are so horrid enough for society to come together and form a general consensus. Such observation could be seen as dismissive of the minority who go against the majority. If deeming something as an "aesthetic failure" seems crude, then I beg the question - what would you think of someone who believed that 9 + 10 truly equaled to 21?

What are characteristics of bad menswear..?

It's important to separate subjective opinions from more broad ideas that make menswear bad. For example, garment fits: full cut, straight cut, American/British/Italian suiting, etc. Such things are strongly based in preference and shouldn't be seen as a definitive answer to bad menswear (though, it can be if the fit is ill enough). Of course, this extends to much other factors such as the garment design, patterning, coloring, etc. To demonstrate what bad menswear can looklike, refer to the picture below.

Essentially, this looks like a costume. The overly large striped pattern with a silly color palette combined with a poor fitting jacket screams October.
